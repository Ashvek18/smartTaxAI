I have built and configured the SmartTaxAI full-stack tax planning and investment optimization application. The platform provides a comprehensive set of computational tax tools tailored to latest Indian Income Tax regulations.
Here is a summary of the implemented modules and architectural features:
💼 Core Technical features & Layout Modules
Secure Session Gateway: Implemented robust full-stack JWT validation with instant sandbox configurations (user@smarttax.ai / user123 and administrator admin@smarttax.ai / admin123).
Indian Income Tax Surcharges & Rebates Engine: Created a mathematically precise compliance engine calculating House Rent Allowance (HRA) exemptions under Sec 10(13A) (least-of Basic/Rent rules adapting metro-vs-non-metro limits) alongside standard Section 87A Rebates and 4% Educational Cess.
Regime Side-by-Side Comparison Terminal: Displays side-by-side ledgers matching Old vs New Regimes, showing a complete breakdown of slabs, base obligations, and total savings.
Visual Deduction Shield Planners & Checklist Guides: Integrated interactive sliders claiming Section 80C indexes, Section 80D medical covers, and Section 80CCD(1B) NPS limits, indicating remaining optimization capacity.
AdvisoryMATCH & AI Financial CA: Suggests highly calibrated investment schemes matching lock-ins (ELSS 3Y, PPF 15Y, NPS up to Retirement), annualized CAGR yields, and tax shields. Integrated server-side Gemini 2.5 Flash chat with contextual tax expertise.
TDS Timeline Forecasting & Goal tracking: Forecasts month-by-month TDS trends while allowing creation and tracking of long-term milestones (Property, Retirement, Vehicles).
Global Administration Console: Allows administrators to inspect security audit logs, manage database investment catalogs, inspect sandbox registered members, and calibrate global default IT Act slab standard deductions.
Export Document Simulator: Features dynamic generation and download protocols for salary/tax schedules in CSV/Text formats.

=========================


Build a production-ready full-stack web application called SmartTaxAI that helps salaried employees optimize taxes, compare tax regimes, and plan investments for maximum savings.

Objective

Create a platform that simplifies tax planning by analyzing a user's salary structure, deductions, investments, and financial goals to provide actionable recommendations and projected tax savings.

The application should solve real-world problems faced by Indian salaried employees while complying with the latest Indian Income Tax regulations.
